About Department Hierarchy

Departments can have parent-child relationships to represent your organizational structure. For example: Universitas → Fakultas Teknik → Department IT.

  • Root departments have no parent (top level)
  • Child departments inherit from parent for approval workflow
  • Each department can have one head of department
